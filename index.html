About the event

APSSDC (Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation) is a state government initiative aimed at promoting skill development and entrepreneurship among the youth of Andhra Pradesh. Established in 2015, APSSDC focuses on providing vocational training and certification programs in various sectors, such as IT, manufacturing, and services. The organization also supports startups and entrepreneurs through incubation, mentorship, and funding. Additionally, APSSDC collaborates with industries to create job opportunities and provide on-the-job training. PMKVY (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana) is a national initiative launched by the Government of India in 2015. The scheme aims to provide skill training and certification to Indian youth, focusing on employability and entrepreneurship. PMKVY also recognizes and certifies skills acquired through informal learning or work experience, and facilitates job placement for trained candidates. The program offers training in various sectors, including agriculture, automotive, beauty and wellness, construction, electronics, healthcare, IT and ITES, logistics, manufacturing, retail, and telecommunications. Both APSSDC and PMKVY aim to bridge the skill gap in India and create a workforce that is industry-ready and globallyÂ competitive.
